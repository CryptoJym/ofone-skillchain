# Case: Public-Sector AI Policy Audit

## Case ID

`case-public-sector-ai-policy-audit-001`

## Domain Mix

- strategic-agentic
- scientific-explanatory
- normative-evaluative
- hybrid

## Prompt

A public-sector organization is considering an AI-assisted decision process with performance, rights, legitimacy, review, and operational constraints. Produce a decision map that keeps model evidence, policy claims, stakeholder exposure, gates, and review logs separate.

## Expected OfOne Pressure Points

- rights and legitimacy gates
- stakeholder and reviewer assignment
- model-performance evidence gaps
- dissent and contention visibility
- review-log handling for approved or blocked gates

## Baselines

- direct policy recommendation
- light structured risk review
- full OfOne artifact and Audit rendering
