# Case: Scientific Mechanism Check

## Case ID

`case-scientific-mechanism-check-001`

## Domain Mix

- scientific-explanatory

## Prompt

A team has a plausible mechanism, limited measurements, possible confounding, and a proposed intervention. Produce a decision-ready map that separates observations, mechanism claims, hidden variables, measurement gaps, kill tests, and update triggers.

## Expected OfOne Pressure Points

- measurement versus mechanism separation
- confounder and hidden-variable visibility
- testability and replication pressure
- evidence validity windows
- update behavior when new measurement contradicts the mechanism

## Baselines

- direct scientific explanation
- light structured mechanism review
- full OfOne artifact and rendering
